Mom's MeatLoaf

Description

This is the only meatloaf I ever liked! Seasoned with fresh onion,a touch of bell pepper, and glazed with a sweet ketchup sauce. For our small family, we cook one loaf and freeze the other to cook at a later time.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 1/2 cup of crushed saltine crackers
  • 1(8 ounce) can of tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 cup ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Combine Ground Beef into two eqaually sized loaves with mixed ingredients and place on backing dish to heat in stove
  3. Remove dish from oven and continue baking until loaves are no longer pink in the center and let it sit for 20 minutes.
